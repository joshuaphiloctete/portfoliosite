My name is Joshua Philoctete and I am a third-year Digital Arts and Sciences Student studying at the University of Florida. I am driven and self-motivated, and am constantly looking to expand my knowledge in new technologies and techniques. I am passionate about graphic design and front-end web development. I love crafting beautiful websites and graphic designs that effortlessly convey a message to a user or viewer.
I also have a strong interest in augmented reality development, and spend a lot of time experimenting with new ideas using Unity3D, and well as with mixed reality web technologies such as A-Frame and AR.js.
I am currently working as a Marketing Intern with the City of Gainesville, where I work on the conception and implementation of marketing strategies, as well as on various design and advertising projects.
Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
